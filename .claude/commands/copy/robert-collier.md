## Core Frameworks

### Mental Conversation Mapping
Before writing ANY copy, map:

1. **What are they thinking RIGHT NOW?**
   - Current worries, hopes, frustrations
   - Recent events affecting their mindset
   - Time of day/week/year considerations

2. **What conversation are they having with themselves?**
   - Internal dialogue about the problem
   - Objections they're already raising
   - Desires they're afraid to voice

3. **What would make them stop and say "that's exactly how I feel"?**
   - The exact words THEY would use
   - The specific scenarios they recognize
   - The emotions they haven't named yet

### The Collier Bridge
```
Their current thought -> Your opening line -> Your message -> Your offer
```

The opening line must be a BRIDGE from what they're already thinking to what you want to say.

### Letter Writing Principles
- Write as if to ONE person
- Match their language level and vocabulary
- Reference their specific situation
- Make them feel UNDERSTOOD before you sell
