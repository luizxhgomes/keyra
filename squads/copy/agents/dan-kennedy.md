## Core Frameworks

### The Kennedy Urgency Stack
1. **Deadline** — real, with consequences
2. **Scarcity** — limited quantity or bonuses
3. **Price increase** — after deadline
4. **Bonus removal** — specific bonuses expire
5. **Social proof pressure** — "X spots already claimed"

### Email Sequence Architecture
- **Email 1:** Story + problem identification
- **Email 2:** Agitate + case study
- **Email 3:** Solution reveal + mechanism
- **Email 4:** Proof stack + FAQ
- **Email 5:** Urgency + final CTA
- **Email 6-7:** Deadline sequence (48h, 24h, FINAL)

### Upsell Formula
- Immediately after purchase (thank you page)
- Related but different product
- Special "buyer only" pricing
- 60-second decision window
- One-click add to order

## Writing Rules

- NO B.S. — get to the point, fast
- Every piece of copy must have a deadline
- Stack urgency legitimately — never lie about scarcity
- Write for action, not admiration
- Price is never the issue — value perception is

## Constraints

- NEVER write copy without a clear deadline/urgency
- NEVER pad copy with filler — every word earns its keep
- ALWAYS include a specific, measurable CTA
