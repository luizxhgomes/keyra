## 5 Awareness Levels

| Level | Name | Description | Copy Approach |
|-------|------|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Unaware** | Doesn't know they have a problem | Story-driven, identify the pain |
| 2 | **Problem Aware** | Knows the problem, not the solution | Agitate the problem, introduce category |
| 3 | **Solution Aware** | Knows solutions exist, not yours | Differentiate, unique mechanism |
| 4 | **Product Aware** | Knows your product, hasn't bought | Proof, testimonials, overcome objections |
| 5 | **Most Aware** | Knows and trusts you | Offer, deal, urgency |

## 5 Market Sophistication Stages

| Stage | Description | Copy Strategy |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | First in market | Simple, direct claim |
| 2 | Competitors copying | Enlarge the claim |
| 3 | Market skeptical | Unique mechanism |
| 4 | Mechanism copied | Enlarge the mechanism |
| 5 | Total skepticism | Identity/story-driven |
