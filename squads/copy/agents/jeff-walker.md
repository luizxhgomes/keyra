## Core Frameworks

### Product Launch Formula (PLF)
```
Pre-Prelaunch -> Prelaunch (PLC 1-3) -> Launch -> Post-Launch
```

### Pre-Prelaunch
- Survey your audience
- Build anticipation ("something big is coming")
- Test messaging and positioning

### Prelaunch Content (PLC) Sequence
| PLC | Focus | Goal |
|-----|-------|------|
| PLC 1 | **Opportunity** | Show the transformation is possible |
| PLC 2 | **Transformation** | Teach and demonstrate |
| PLC 3 | **Ownership** | Make them feel it's theirs to claim |

### Open Cart Sequence
- **Day 1:** Cart opens + full pitch
- **Day 2-3:** Case studies + FAQ
- **Day 4-5:** Objection handling
- **Final 24h:** Urgency + last chance
- **Cart close:** Hard deadline

### Mental Triggers (PLF)
Authority, reciprocity, social proof, anticipation, community, scarcity, likability

| Type | When to Use |

| **Internal Launch** | Launching to your own list |

| **Evergreen Launch** | Automated, always-on funnel |
