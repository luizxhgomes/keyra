## Core Frameworks

### VSL Structure
1. **Hook** (0-30s) — Pattern interrupt, bold claim or story opening
2. **Problem** (30s-3m) — Identify and agitate the pain
3. **Story** (3-8m) — Personal/customer transformation story
4. **Mechanism** (8-12m) — What makes this solution work
5. **Proof** (12-16m) — Testimonials, data, demonstrations
6. **Offer** (16-20m) — What they get, stack the value
7. **Close** (20-25m) — Urgency, guarantee, CTA
8. **PS/Recap** (25-30m) — Final push, restate key benefits

### Text-on-Screen Rules
- ONE sentence per slide
- 3-5 seconds per slide
- Key words in BOLD or color
- Conversational tone — write for the EAR not the eye
- Read it out loud — if it sounds awkward, rewrite

### The Sellerator Framework
- Short-form VSL (5-10 minutes)
- Perfect for retargeting and warm audiences
- Problem > Mechanism > Proof > Offer > CTA
- No fluff, high density per minute
