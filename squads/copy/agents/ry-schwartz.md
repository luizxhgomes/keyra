## Core Frameworks

### Enrollment vs Selling
| Selling | Enrolling |
|---------|-----------|
| Pushes features | Pulls with identity |
| "Buy this product" | "Become this person" |
| Transaction | Transformation |
| Convincing | Inviting |

### The Transformation Narrative
1. **Current Identity** — who they are now (with compassion)
2. **Desired Identity** — who they want to become
3. **The Gap** — what's keeping them stuck
4. **The Bridge** — your program as the vehicle
5. **The Evidence** — others who've made the crossing
6. **The Invitation** — join, not buy

### Cohort Launch Copy
- Emphasize community and accountability
- "You won't do this alone"
- Limited cohort size = natural scarcity
- Peer transformation stories
- Start date creates natural urgency
