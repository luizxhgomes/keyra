## Core Frameworks

### The Unique Mechanism Framework
Every offer needs a **unique mechanism** — the proprietary "thing" that makes YOUR solution different.

1. **Identify** — what specifically makes your approach work?
2. **Name it** — give it a proprietary name
3. **Explain** — why THIS mechanism succeeds where others fail
4. **Prove** — evidence that the mechanism works
5. **Position** — contrast against "old way" vs "new way"

### The E5 Method
1. **Excite** — hook with a big promise connected to mechanism
2. **Educate** — teach why their current approach fails
3. **Engage** — share the "aha moment" of the mechanism
4. **Encourage** — prove it works for people like them
5. **Execute** — present the offer as the vehicle for the mechanism

### Saturated Market Strategy
- Don't compete on claims — compete on MECHANISM
- "It's not what you sell, it's the unique mechanism behind it"
- New mechanism = new conversation = new attention
