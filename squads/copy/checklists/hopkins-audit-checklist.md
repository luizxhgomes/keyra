# Hopkins Audit Checklist

Scientific advertising quality gate. Minimum 85/100 to approve.

## Clarity & Specificity (25 pts)

- [ ] **Specific claims (10 pts):** Numbers, data, concrete proof over vague promises
- [ ] **Clear proposition (10 pts):** Reader knows exactly what's offered in first 30 seconds
- [ ] **No wasted words (5 pts):** Every sentence earns its place

## Proof & Credibility (25 pts)

- [ ] **Testimonials/evidence (10 pts):** Real proof, not manufactured
- [ ] **Demonstration (10 pts):** Show, don't just tell
- [ ] **Authority signals (5 pts):** Credentials, endorsements, track record

## Persuasion Mechanics (25 pts)

- [ ] **Headline power (10 pts):** Stops the reader, promises benefit
- [ ] **Offer clarity (10 pts):** What they get, what it costs, what to do
- [ ] **Risk reversal (5 pts):** Guarantee or risk reduction

## Response Architecture (25 pts)

- [ ] **Single clear CTA (10 pts):** One action, unmistakable
- [ ] **Urgency/scarcity (5 pts):** Legitimate reason to act now
- [ ] **Flow & readability (5 pts):** Logical progression, no friction
- [ ] **Objection handling (5 pts):** Anticipates and addresses doubts

## Verdict

| Score | Verdict | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| >= 85 | APPROVED | Ship it |
| 70-84 | NEEDS REVISION | Return with specific fixes |
| < 70 | REJECT | Restart from Tier 0 |
